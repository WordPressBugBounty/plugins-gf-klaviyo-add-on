Gravity Forms Klaviyo Add-On seamlessly integrates Gravity Forms with Klaviyo, enabling powerful email marketing automation.

== Description ==

Supercharge your email marketing with [Klaviyo](https://www.klaviyo.com/) and Gravity Forms!

### Free Version Features:

The **Gravity Forms Klaviyo Add-On** enables you to seamlessly connect your Gravity Forms with [Klaviyo](https://www.klaviyo.com/), a powerful email marketing platform. The free version offers basic integration capabilities, including:

- **Basic Contact Sync**: Automatically send form submissions from Gravity Forms to Klaviyo, capturing essential contact information for your email marketing campaigns.
- **Limited Field Mapping**: Map only the **Name** and **Email** fields from Gravity Forms to Klaviyo to start building your contact list.
- **Single List Addition**: Add new contacts to a specific Klaviyo list directly from your Gravity Forms submissions.

This free version is perfect for users looking to get started with basic email marketing integrations using Gravity Forms and Klaviyo.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/gf-klaviyo-add-on` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Go to Forms > Settings > Klaviyo to configure the plugin.
